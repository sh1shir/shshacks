About Us

MicroBiz is a website that promotes small businesses during this unprecedented time. Small businesses have been impacted as much as result of the recent COVID-19 pandemic, but many comapnies have been struggling for years, and this pandemic just exacerbates the problem. The most common reasons small businesses fail include a lack of capital or funding, retaining an inadequate management team, a faulty infrastructure or business model, and unsuccessful marketing initiatives. Through this website, we hope to draw attention to these small business and support their economic needs.
